# 0.1.0 — 2026-06

Initial release of AI Interview Assistant.

## Added

- **Live session assistant.** Real-time transcription of the interviewer's voice
  (system-audio loopback) with streaming AI answers.
- **Floating overlay.** A always-on-top answer panel with click-through and a
  compact/expanded layout, toggled by a global shortcut.
- **Quick & region solve.** Answer a problem from the clipboard, or drag-select a
  screen region to read and answer it.
- **Profiles.** Create and edit candidate profiles, including job-description
  context with a website-research fallback.
- **Mock interviews & reports.** Practice mode plus saved reports of past sessions.
- **Guided tour** for first-time users.
- **Privacy Mode.** Hide the app from screen sharing and recording (on by default).
- **Global shortcuts** for overlay, quick/region solve, pause/resume, privacy, and
  overlay click-through.

## Notes

- Hybrid-GPU escape hatch: launch with `--disable-gpu` (or `AI_DISABLE_GPU=1`) on
  NVIDIA Optimus laptops where the window may otherwise fail to display.
