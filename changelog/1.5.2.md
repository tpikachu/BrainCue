# 1.5.2 — 2026-07-15

**The real Privacy Mode fix.** (v1.5.1's attempt didn't hold — this one is verified.)

## Fixed

- **Privacy Mode now stays on when you interact with the window.** The root cause:
  on Windows, **clicking, focusing, moving, or resizing** a window silently clears
  the screen-capture exclusion — so the moment you touched the Cue Card or the
  dashboard, it reappeared in your screen share, even though Privacy Mode still
  said it was on. BrainCue now **re-asserts the exclusion on every focus, click,
  move, resize, and restore**, backed by a continuous watchdog that re-applies it
  a few times a second while Privacy Mode is on — so the app stays hidden through
  any interaction.

  Verified head-to-head against the same capture technology Zoom/Meet/Teams use:
  before the fix, clicking the window made it appear in the capture; after the fix,
  it stays hidden through clicking, dragging, and resizing.

**If you use Privacy Mode, please update — and do a 10-second check after updating:**
share your screen in a meeting app, then click and drag BrainCue's windows and
confirm they don't appear.
